DESCRIPTION

Burger is a popular food choice in the western culture and is among the top choices in the fast food category.

Due to it's huge popularity among the people many experiments have been done with it to attract the fast food loving community.

Since the ingredients of a burger mainly depend upon the choice of the consumer there are not any fixed ingredients for it . So today we are going to introduce you to the basic ingredients that were initially used in a burger .

"Ingredients"

  • Sliced Bun
  • Cheese
  • Veg/Non-veg Patty
  • Lettuce
  • Onion
  • Tomato

"Steps"

  1. Take a slice bun and place it face up on the table.
  2. Put a patty of your choice on the bun.
  3. Put the cheese slice.
  4. Now lettuce.
  5. Then Onion.
  6. Then Tomato.
  7. Place the other half of the bun on the tomato face down.
  8. Put the result in a microwave for 5 minutes.
  9. Now enjoy your delicious treat.

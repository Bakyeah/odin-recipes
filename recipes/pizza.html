DESCRIPTION

From being a common street food to becoming one of the top listers in the fast food category .

Pizza was first introduced in Italy and easily became an integral part of the food community.

Even before it's creation street vendors in Italy sold plain bread with many sort of toppings so the exact time of it's invention is nearly impossible to trace.

Pizza is made from the combination of plain dough garnished with many sort of toppings and sauces . The combination of all these toppings in turn give it an out of this world taste helping it make its place among the top listers .

"Ingredients"

  • Flour
  • Cheese
  • Salt
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Onion
  • Tomato

    • "Steps"

    1. Make dough of required consistency using the flour .
    2. Add a little salt to the dough.
    3. Flatten the dough .
    4. Spread tomato Sauce over the result using the back of a spoon.
    5. Spread grated cheese over it.
    6. Top with onion slices and tomatos.
    7. Put it inside an oven for 10-15 minutes.
    8. Take out the Pizza and enjoy.
